The Bride

Patricia

Patricia, born in Saxony, quickly left her small home town in search of new horizons.
First going to Magdeburg to study sign language, she eventually found a home in Berlin and more recently completed a 5 month DTS with YWAM in Australia and got a chance to meet Jonathan's family in New Zealand.
Patricia loves a good book, loves her job, good friends and singing at her church Equippers.

The Groom

Jonathan

A migrant with a heart as big as the man himself. Apparently he thought his bride was worth more than the New Zealand scenery, Burgerfuel and his own car. He left these all behind to embrace the German precision, and to develop the world from his job at "HERE maps", right in the heart of Berlin. He got use to the topsy-turvyness of the bride's family very quickly and gained an interest in board games and good German food.

Love at fist sight

The first flames lit up at a couple of brief encounters at the Equippers church. At a lunch, to which he actually invited another friend, who coincidently didn't show up, the sparks jumped quickly. Despite the difference in mother tongue and the nervous tension, which kept Patricia from eating, and lead to a few synaptic pathway malfunctions, they quickly learned to jump the barriers. Already used to a long distance relationship between them, they successfully navigated a 14,569.19 km distance, and nothing could split them as a team.
