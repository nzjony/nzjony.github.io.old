In between

While the Bride and Groom deal with the Paparazzi, between the church and evening celebration, you have the chance to stretch your legs.
The coming of spring invites you to go for a walk in the famous Park Sanssouci. Or explore the beautiful city of Potsdam, especially the streets around "Luisenplatz". Here you will find many opportunities for a snack. For those wanting to go straight to the celebration hall, if you are staying there, feel free to nap or enjoy the coffee and cake that is waiting there for you. Regarding this, we have a request, it would be great, especially those from Berlin, if you could bring some baking to sweeten your waiting time. If you plan for this, a short message to the Bride would be good. Thanks a lot!
